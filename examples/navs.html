About HealthWatch

HealthWatch.se

HealthWatch.se offers scientifically based tools for modern, proactive, healthy and fact-based organizational development. The system includes simple, scientifically proven and time-efficient resources to maintain and improve health and wellbeing as well as ensuring good and healthy work environment. HealthWatch.se targets the whole organization, and contains features and tools for individuals, managers and management/HR.

Systematic and proactive health promotion

HealthWatch.se is completely automated and is built on interactive self-help and self-learning technology. It means that you are provided with instant feedback, tailored action plans as well as self-help and educational tools in different formats to suit different learning styles and preferences. These tools are directed at the individual, group and organizational level to ensure intervention from a holistic perspective.
